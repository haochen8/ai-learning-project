## Capstone

Build a small agentic workflow with logging, replay, eval criteria, retrieval or memory, and a human-visible output layer.

Deliverables:
- system diagram
- prompt/tool contract
- eval set
- failure-mode log
- one-page reflection on what should remain deterministic
